### Stock Analysis of Market Hours moves vs Non Market Hours

I was curious to find out if most stock moves come during market hours or from the non market hours and gaps between yesterday's closing price and next days opening price. Using Apple stock with the ticker AAPL as a example, I analyze the data to find the truth.
